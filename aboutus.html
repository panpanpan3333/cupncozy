ABOUT US

At Cup and Cozy, we believe in the simple joy of a warm drink in a cozy space. Our mission is to offer more than just great coffee and tea — we create a place where you can unwind, relax, and enjoy the moment. From handcrafted beverages to freshly baked treats, we're here to make your experience as comforting as a hug in a cup. Whether you're here for a quick break or to linger, we invite you to find your cozy spot with us.

Cozy and Comfy cafe Shop

The Cup and Cozy café is a warm and inviting sanctuary, designed to provide a comforting escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The space feels like an extension of home—intimate, relaxed, and perfect for slowing down. The style is a blend of rustic charm and modern comfort, with elements that make every corner feel like a cozy retreat.
